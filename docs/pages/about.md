# About this site

This site publishes a curated, sanitised subset of work completed during the
**Master of Applied Cybernetics (MACYB)** at the Australian National University.

## Scope — what will be published

- Sanitised source code and technical documentation
- Project overviews, architecture diagrams, and methodology notes
- Small illustrative data samples (synthetic or de-identified)
- Blog posts on topics and themes explored during the programme

## Scope — what will not be published

The following categories of content are intentionally excluded:

- **Assessment submissions** — reports, essays, exams, or any graded work
- **Raw personal reflections** — journal entries or private notes
- **Sensitive data** — anything that could identify peers, staff, or third parties
- **Large or raw datasets** — only small, sanitised samples are included
- **Any confidential or proprietary material**

## Sanitisation statement

Before any artefact is published here it is reviewed to remove or redact
identifying information, confidential content, and any material that should
remain private. When in doubt, content is excluded rather than published.

## Contact

Raise an issue in this repository if you have questions or concerns about
published content.
