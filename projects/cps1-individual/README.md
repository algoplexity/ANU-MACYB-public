# CPS1 — Individual Project

Placeholder for sanitised artefacts from the CPS1 individual project,
completed as part of the **Master of Applied Cybernetics (MACYB)** at the
Australian National University.

## What will be published here

- Sanitised source code (`src/`)
- Technical documentation and write-ups (`docs/`)
- Small, de-identified or synthetic data samples (`data/samples/`)

## What will not be published

- Raw or full datasets
- Assessment submissions or graded reports
- Any sensitive, confidential, or identifying information

Content will be added once it has been reviewed and sanitised.
